Qr Code e Barcode Scanner


Contents:

With this app you can scan Qr codes and barcodes. Here's how to use it:
The most important thing for scanning is that the code has a good resolution, is not blurred and not tilted.
Avoid light reflections or shadows on the code.
The code does not have to fill the entire scan area. Do not hold the camera too close, as the code may become out of focus.
Likewise, increasing the scanning area can help with very large, complex, or very long codes.
